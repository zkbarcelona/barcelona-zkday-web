zkDay Barcelona - April 22$^\text{nd}$, 2024

Description

zkDay Barcelona aims to be a full day dedicated to zero-knowledge cryptography and its applications. The event will feature a series of talks by leading experts in the field, covering a wide range of topics from the theoretical foundations of zero-knowledge proofs to the latest developments in the area. The event will also provide an opportunity for networking and collaboration among researchers and practitioners working on zero-knowledge cryptography.

Event topics

  • Zero-Knowledge Cryptography
  • Proving Systems
  • Probabilistic Proof Systems
  • Folding Schemas
